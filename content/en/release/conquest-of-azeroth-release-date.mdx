For example, the Sun Cleric and the Cultist offer thematic mirrors to traditional priests, while the Tinker brings a long-requested engineering-based combat style to the world of Azeroth.

## Reimagined World Features and Mechanics

Beyond the classes, Conquest of Azeroth introduces several mechanical changes that differentiate it from a standard Vanilla WoW experience. The goal was to create a world that feels familiar but plays like a modern RPG. This includes a total overhaul of the looting system, where gear can be looted directly from the specific mobs you are fighting, rather than relying solely on static loot tables.

The world itself has been expanded. While the continents of the Eastern Kingdoms and Kalimdor remain the primary setting, many zones have been updated with new creatures, extra stats on items, and expanded questlines. This focus on the "old world" is a deliberate choice to keep the adventure grounded in the lore of Azeroth rather than venturing into cosmic realms.

| Feature | Description | Impact on Gameplay |
