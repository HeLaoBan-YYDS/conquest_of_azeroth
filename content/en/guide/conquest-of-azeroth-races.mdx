## What is Conquest of Azeroth?

Conquest of Azeroth (CoA) is a specialized server hosted by the Project Ascension team. Unlike their famous "Classless" realms like Bronzebeard, CoA features a fixed-class system—but with a massive twist. Instead of the original nine classes found in the 1-60 Vanilla world, players can choose from 21 entirely custom classes. These classes feature unique spells, custom animations, and complex resource systems that go far beyond standard mana or rage.

According to player experience and community reports, the server functions as a "Classic Plus" environment that utilizes the 1-60 world but revamps almost every mechanical aspect. While it takes place in the familiar geography of Azeroth, the gameplay is significantly faster and more modern, utilizing a talent tree system inspired by the Dragonflight expansion. This allows for immense customization within each of the 21 archetypes.

## The Role of Conquest of Azeroth Races

In a standard WoW environment, your choice of race often comes down to a single powerful racial ability, such as the Orc’s Blood Fury or the Human’s Perception. However, in this custom environment, the **conquest of azeroth races** take on new importance because they must support classes that didn't exist in the original game.

Whether you are playing a Tinker, a Necromancer, or a Venommancer, your racial choice can provide the edge needed in high-stakes PvE or PvP. Community reports suggest that while the racial abilities remain largely grounded in Vanilla traditions, their interactions with custom resources like "Insanity" or "Scrap" create entirely new gameplay loops.

## Deep Dive: 21 Custom Classes and Race Selection

Choosing from the **conquest of azeroth races** is only half the battle; the real depth lies in the 21 custom classes. These classes are divided into various roles, including traditional DPS, tanks, and healers, but many utilize hybrid mechanics that allow them to swap roles based on their talent tree.

### The Tinker: A Master of Mechanics
The Tinker is one of the most celebrated additions to the server. Based on the Warcraft 3 hero and the engineering profession, this class allows players to use robots, turrets, and mechs. 

*   **Mechanics (DPS):** Focuses on turrets and a permanent pet robot. At high scrap levels, you can transform into a giant Shredder mech.
*   **Invention (Healer):** Uses nanobots and stim shots to heal allies. This spec can place battery stations that act similarly to a Priest's Lightwell.
*   **Demolition (AoE DPS):** All about explosives, landmines, and rocket launchers.

For the Tinker, Gnomes and Dwarves are the thematic and mechanical favorites on the Alliance side, while Goblins (often represented through custom skins or Trolls) dominate the Horde meta.

### The Venommancer and Witch Doctor
These classes lean heavily into the "Troll" aesthetic and lore. The Venommancer acts as a "Poison Druid," allowing players to shapeshift into spiders, scorpions, or snakes. They serve as disciples of Shadra the Loa. 

The Witch Doctor, on the other hand, is a master of hexes and curses. They can even summon primal dinosaurs to aid them in combat. If you are looking at **conquest of azeroth races** for these classes, Trolls are the undisputed kings due to the thematic synergy and the Berserking racial, which speeds up hex casting.

### Blood Mage and Necromancer
The Blood Mage is a unique "high-risk, high-reward" class. It uses health as a primary resource, allowing the player to transition between a caster form and a powerful Worgen-like melee form once enough blood has been "harvested." 

The Necromancer is a significant departure from the standard Warlock or Death Knight. It allows for the summoning of multiple undead pets simultaneously, creating a "pet swarm" playstyle that requires careful management of essence.

## Revamped Starting Zones and Leveling

When you select one of the **conquest of azeroth races**, you aren't just entering the old 2004 version of Northshire or Durotar. The Ascension team has revamped the starting experiences to include new quests, immersive world-forged item drops, and faster combat pacing.

Player experiences suggest that the leveling process is much smoother than traditional Vanilla WoW. You will encounter "World Forge" items—powerful gear with randomized stats—much earlier in your journey. This makes the initial 1-20 grind feel rewarding and keeps the gameplay loop fresh as you experiment with your custom talent trees.

## The Dragonflight Talent System in CoA

One of the most modern features of the server is the implementation of the "Dragonflight" style talent trees. This system replaces the old three-pane Vanilla trees with a highly customizable, branching path system. 

For every class associated with the **conquest of azeroth races**, this means you can build highly specific "sub-specs." For example, a Tinker could invest heavily into the "Demolition" tree for explosive damage but take a few utility points from the "Invention" tree to provide minor healing to their pet robot. This level of customization ensures that even two players of the same race and class might play entirely differently.

## How to Access Conquest of Azeroth

As of mid-2026, Conquest of Azeroth is still in a prolonged testing phase, often referred to as the Alpha or PTR (Public Test Realm). To play, you generally need to follow these steps:

1.  **Download the Launcher:** Visit the [official Project Ascension website](https://ascension.gg/) and download their custom client.
2.  **Select the PTR:** In the dropdown menu, change your realm selection to the PTR/Conquest of Azeroth client.
3.  **Obtain the Book:** Access to this specific realm often requires a "Conquest of Azeroth Guide Book." These can be purchased from the in-game shop or acquired through specific events on the main Bronzebeard or Al'ar servers.
4.  **Create Your Character:** Select from the available **conquest of azeroth races** and begin your journey through the custom 1-60 world.

Community reports indicate that while the server has faced stability issues in the past, recent updates have significantly improved server uptime and reduced crashes. However, because it is essentially a "PTR for new content," expect frequent balance changes as the developers try to manage the 21-class ecosystem.

## Balancing and the Future of CoA

One of the biggest concerns voiced by the community is the "impossible" task of balancing 21 custom classes. With unique resources like nanobots, blood-bending, and primal dinosaur summons, the PvP meta can shift wildly from week to week. 

However, the developers have maintained that CoA is a "fun-first" server. The goal isn't necessarily the perfect balance found in retail WoW, but rather a playground for class fantasy. The amount of customization available through the **conquest of azeroth races** ensures that players can always find a niche, even if their specific spec isn't at the top of the DPS meters.

Looking forward to late 2026 and early 2027, the community expects a full "release" date. Until then, the server remains a high-quality "short burst" of fun for those tired of the standard WoW formula and looking for something truly "insane."

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which of the conquest of azeroth races is best for a beginner?
For players new to the server, the **Human** or **Orc** races are generally the safest bets. They offer well-rounded racial abilities that benefit almost all 21 custom classes. Humans are particularly good for classes that consume a lot of resources due to their Spirit bonus, while Orcs provide a reliable damage boost for melee-focused custom classes.

### Do I need a special client to play these races?
Yes, you must use the Project Ascension launcher. Within the launcher, you need to select the "PTR" or "Conquest of Azeroth" option. Additionally, playing on this realm usually requires a specific "CoA Book" which can be obtained through their website or in-game marketplace on their other servers.

### Can I play as a custom race in Conquest of Azeroth?
Currently, the **conquest of azeroth races** are based on the core Vanilla WoW roster (Humans, Orcs, Dwarves, Trolls, etc.). However, the server features "World Forge" items and custom skins that can significantly alter your character's appearance, effectively allowing you to roleplay as sub-races or entirely different creatures while maintaining the base racial stats.

### How do the custom classes interact with racial abilities?
Racial abilities have been tuned to interact with the new class resources. For example, a Troll's "Berserking" might increase the rate at which a Tinker generates "Scrap" or how fast a Necromancer can harvest "Essence." Choosing a race that complements your class's primary resource is the key to high-level optimization.
