## Understanding Vanity Syncs and Server Seasons

Project Ascension operates on a seasonal model. When a seasonal server (such as Elune) concludes, characters and their vanity collections are merged into the evergreen realm, Area 52. When a new seasonal realm or a major project like Conquest of Azeroth launches, players typically must purchase a "Vanity Sync" to copy their cosmetic mounts, pets, and titles from the shared account collection to the new realm.

For the Conquest of Azeroth launch, player experiences confirm that a Vanity Sync option will be available in the store. This allows veteran players to bring their hard-earned cosmetics over to the new server. 

If you purchased the Travel Guide, you do not need to worry about buying a sync just to get your bundle rewards; those specific items (like the Cyclopian Dread and the Founder Title) will be waiting for you on your account when the live server goes online. For all other account-wide cosmetics, you will need to purchase a standard Vanity Sync.
